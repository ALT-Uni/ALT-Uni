CLOSING THE GAP.
Currently there is a well documented gap in the labour market for ICT (Information and Communication Technology). Simple out, there are more jobs in the ICT industry than there are skilled people to fill them

What is the Call to Action - What's the hiney pot?

WHAT IS THE GAP?
In New Zealand there are several industries with labour shortages. Simply put, there are more jobs than there are people to fill them.

One of these industries is ICT, or Information Communication Technology. This encompasses areas such as development, IT systems, television, radio, hardware and software, cloud tech and more. Broad right? Clearly we don’t have enough tech heads in the world to do these jobs.

But this isn’t true. We do have enough people. People like you - with a potentially untapped passion for code, computers or analysis - could fill this need, close the gap, and find a fulfilling career in the meantime. WHY ICT?

WHY ICT?
ICT offers exciting career options for the right person. Technology is evolving all the time, which means that there are constantly emerging opportunities and jobs in this industry. Careers in tech and IT also have some of the highest paying annual salaries in New Zealand.
Use the resources on Fill The Gap to explore your options, discover new passions, to learn new skills, and to see if ICT is the path for you.
Have some skills already and want to test them in the real world? We have a number of internships, opportunities and events listed on the Work page.
